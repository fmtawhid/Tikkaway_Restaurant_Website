About Us

Welcome to Tikkaway

Tikkaway is a restaurant that specializes in Indian cuisine and is located in the Dhaka. The restaurant offers a unique and modern twist on traditional Bangali and Cainis dishes, with a focus on providing fast and healthy meals for people on-the-go. One of the key features of Tikkaway is its build-your-own-bowl concept, where customers can choose from a variety of proteins, sauces, and toppings to create their own custom meal. Some of the popular proteins offered include chicken tikka, paneer, and lamb kebab, and customers can choose from a range of sauces like tikka masala, vindaloo, and korma. Toppings include vegetables like peppers, onions, and tomatoes, as well as options like chutneys and raita.

In addition to the build-your-own-bowl concept, Tikkaway also offers a range of other Indian dishes, such as biryani, naan, and samosas. The restaurant uses fresh and locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, and strives to provide healthy and flavorful meals to its customers. Tikkaway also offers online ordering and delivery services, making it easy for customers to enjoy their favorite Indian dishes from the comfort of their own home or office. Overall, Tikkaway is a unique and innovative restaurant that offers a fresh take on traditional Indian cuisine.
